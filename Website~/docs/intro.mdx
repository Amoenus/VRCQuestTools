# Introduction

## VRCQuestTools

VRCQuestTools is an editor extension to support uploading VRChat avatars for Oculus Quest.

- You can make your PC avatar uploadable for Quest with a single click.
- You can use various tools to manually make your avatar Quest compatible.

## Quick Guide

### Installation

To add VRCQuestTools to your project, follow these steps:

1. Click the button below to add the repository to VCC.

  <p>
    <Link
      className="button button--primary button--lg"
      to="vcc://vpm/addRepo?url=https://kurotu.github.io/vpm-repos/vpm.json">
      Add to VCC
    </Link>
  </p>

2. Add VRCQuestTools package to your VCC project.

### Convert Avatar for Quest

To convert your avatar for Quest, follow these steps:

1. Right-click your avatar in the scene hierarchy.
2. Select **VRCQuestTools** > **Convert Avatar For Quest** from the context menu.
3. Click **Convert** button in the opened window.
4. Wait for the conversion to complete.
5. Upload the converted avatar for Android platform.
